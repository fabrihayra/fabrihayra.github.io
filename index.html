Analyzing AQI Impacts: Assessing Renewable Subsidy Opportunities for Ripple Renewable Energy

Tasked as a data analyst for Ripple Renewable Energy, this project aims to examine the Air Quality Index (AQI) across six U.S. states in which the company operates. Leveraging data analytics and visualization, the objective is to assess the potential impacts of a proposed federal subsidy for renewables in states with an average AQI above a set threshold. Key deliverables include a summary of state-wise mean AQIs, a boxplot visualization for comparative insights, and confidence interval computations for states with high air quality concerns.
